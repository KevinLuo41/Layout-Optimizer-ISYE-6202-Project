Forward Picking Zone
Analysis Tool


Our project attempts to aid a German food distribution company that needs a tool with capabilities to automatically assign stock keeping units (SKUs) to locations inside a section of a warehouse dedicated to a particular client K1. This tool must also be able to conduct an ABC analysis for the products of client K1.

Developed by @Kaiwen Luo, @Diego Fernandez.
